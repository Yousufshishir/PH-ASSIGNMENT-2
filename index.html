Standard Curriculum
Kinder (3-6) Years

Our early childhood program nurtures curiosity through play-based learning. Children develop social skills, basic numeracy, and creative expression in a supportive environment that prepares them for future academic success.

Elementary School

Building strong foundations in core subjects like math, reading, and science. Our engaging curriculum encourages critical thinking and problem-solving while fostering a love for learning through hands-on activities and collaborative projects.

Middle (10-16) Years

Advanced academic programs that prepare students for higher education. Students explore diverse subjects in depth, develop research skills, and engage in project-based learning while receiving guidance for personal and academic growth.
